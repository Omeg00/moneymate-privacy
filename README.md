# Privacy Policy - MoneyMate

Last updated: May 8, 2026

## Introduction

MoneyMate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data outside of your device.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- All financial data is stored **locally on your device only**
- No analytics or tracking tools
- No advertising networks
- No account registration required
- Works 100% offline

## Data Storage

All data (income, expenses, budgets) is stored in a local database on your device only. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all data.

## Export Feature

CSV and PDF exports are saved to your device and shared only when you manually choose to share them.
